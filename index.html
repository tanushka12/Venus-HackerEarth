    1. RETURN ON INVESTMENT

      • Public transportation boosts property values.
      • Purchasing of new buses will require some kind of investment. The loan will be sanctioned to the bus agency at lower interest rates if the agency has a more certain revenue stream.
      • The organizations normally own valuable land at key locations, in the form of bus depots and large bus stations. The ‘air space’ above this land could be used for commercial purposes. This could bring in substantial revenues in the form of lease/rents.
      • In the long run, investing in public transport will lead to larger economic returns for the country.
